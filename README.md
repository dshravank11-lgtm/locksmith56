# 🧪 What Compound Are You?
A personality quiz that analyzes your responses and tells you what chemical compound matches your vibe. Answer 8 clever questions — 5 multiple choice + 3 open-ended — and get your molecular identity.

# 🧠 How It Works
User answers 8 questions generated by DeepSeek AI

Responses are analyzed to determine a matching chemical compound

Result includes:

Compound name & formula

IUPAC/systematic name

Personality traits

A cheesy pickup line

Wikipedia image of the compound
